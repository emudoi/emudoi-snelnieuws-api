Frequently asked

How do I delete my account?
Open the side menu (top-right hamburger icon on the home screen) and tap Delete Account. Confirm in the dialog. Your account, saved articles, and any data we hold about you will be permanently removed.

Forgot password?
On the sign-in screen, enter your email address and tap Forgot password?. We'll send a password reset link.

Where do the news articles come from?
Articles are aggregated from publicly available news publishers. When you tap into a story, you're taken to the original publisher's website.
